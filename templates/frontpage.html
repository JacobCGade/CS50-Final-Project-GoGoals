Define your own Life Vision


Quote about why having a life vision is important

Who said it, and where to find it

When setting a goal it is important to know why you want to persue exactly that goal. It is important to have a why! To get more motivation and to make sure that the goal is in line with who we want to be and where we want to go, it is a good idea to have a clear life vision and to make goals according to that vision. By doing this you will stay on the right path towards a better you. Successful people who gets admired usually live by principals and values rather than emotions. These principals and values have their roots in your vision, and the greater your vision is based on genuine and righteous laws of nature the more effective and successful you'll be, hence the importance of you vision!

Set Goals and Plans

Quote about the importance of setting goals

Who said it, and where to find it

To set goals is to use a personal navigator set towards a better you. Goals is essentiel for personal development aswell as success in business. Without goals you don't really go anywhere you're just treading waters. But setting goals and especially achieving them isn't as easy as said. We have all tried to set goals or new years resolutions that got thrown in the trash pin in the back of our head and priorities and never got picked up again, or maybe we have worked on and of on the same goal that we never seem to achieve as we give up and slowly forget them, before we then again pick them up. This is where GoGoals comes into the picture. This tool is made to help you set goals and subgoals to make it easier and more fun to go after your dreams. When setting a goal you often need to have subgoals that break that goal down into digestible bites. This makes bigger goals seem achievable and posible. Furthermore it is important to set your goals in accordance to your vision, which will make it personal and motivate you. It also checks rather your goal is in line with who you want to be or not.

With GoGoals all you goals will be stored and viewable in the current goals page. Here you can track your progress and get a reminder of your "why" for during this goal.

See your daily goals and what to focus on

Quote about the importance of repetition or focus

Who said it, and where to find it

The homepage is where you can get an overview of your current goals and what to focus on during the day. This page works as your reminder and guide. You'll be able to see what goals you are currently set to work at, and when you have done them you can mark them as "Done" and move on to the next step. This is the page that you'll want to go to whenever you need to be reminded of what to focus on.

All goals is set with steps to get there. These steps usually take repition, for instance if you need to get a better sleeping rhythm then that will take multiple completions before that habit is set. So whenever you have successfully done something you can mark it as "Done" and GoGoals will remember that and soon that goal will be completed. If you fail at a goal, for instance if you don't get to bed in time, then you should mark the goal as failed and that will have a negative effect in the completion of that specific goal. Maybe you need 10 successes of going to bed at 10 PM then one fail might count as -1 and set you back a bit. Remember repetition is key!
